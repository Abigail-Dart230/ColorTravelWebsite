Mexican Food

Enrich your tastebuds with the delicious food Mexico has to offer

Examples of some food

Here is a list of some Mexican food to try out whilst in the country

  • Huevos Rancheros

    • Start off your morning with a savory breakfreast with some Huevos Rancheros!
    In translation this dish is called 'rancher's eggs'. It is a traditional Mexican breakfast.
    It includes fried eggs, fried corn tortillas, tomtato-chile salsa, beans, cheese, and avocados!

  • Elotes

    • Elotes are a beloved Mexican street food, you can get one on nearly every corner of the street!
    In fact, an "elotero" is a Mexican corn vendor who sells elotes on a cob or cup, you'll find one anywhere in the streets of Mexico.
    An elote is prepared on a grilled corn with mayonnaise, cheese, lime juice and chili powder.

  • Tacos

    • Tacos are not only traditional Mexican food but it is also one of the most iconic!
    You can have tacos for lunch or for dinner, it's up to you completely.
    The wonderful thing about tacos is that it can be anything you want!
    The meat can be any of your choosing like chicken, pork, beef or even shrimp.
    Tacos are usually topped off with lime juice, cilantro leaves, and red onions.
