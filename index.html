Mexico

Mexico, a country full of beauty and culture, is ready to welcome you

What makes it beautiful?

There are many places to see in Mexico, here is a list of some:

  1. Mexico City

    2. The city of Mexico is one of the core places in the entire counrty. It is the cpaital and largest city in Mexico.
    There is always something to do in the city whether that be for work, fun, or simply relaxing.
    It is home to many landmarks such as Palcio de Bellas Artes, a place for all the artists in the world!
    Mexico City is also a great place to meet and befriend locals. There is much to learn from the stories they hold.
    Make sure to say hi to all the dogs you'll encounter on the streets too!

  2. Cancun

    3. Do you want to swim in clear water and sip on a pina colada? Then you should vist Cancun!
    There are so many activities to try out here at Cancun such as parasailing or jet skiing.
    During your stay in Cancun you can also see the gateway of Chichen Itza and Ancient Mayan ruins.
    This place is one of the many gorgeous beaches Mexico has to offer. Tourists are always coming by,
    you could also be one of the many to have fun there!

  3. Teotihuacan

    4. Teotihuacan is located near Mexico City.This place is the ruins of one of the largest cities to have ever existed.
    When you check out the pyrmids of Teptihuacan you are also witnessing Mesoamerican History! In fact. these pyrmids are much older than the Aztecs themselves.
    Teotihuacan is full of impressive pyramids including the Pyramid of the Sun and the Pyramid of the Moon.
    Tourists are allowed to climb up these pyramids too! although some are only allowed to a certain height.
    Come learn some ancient history and purchase handmade jewelry from the vendors nearby!

Aside from these places, there are still many more places to visit when traveling Mexico!
